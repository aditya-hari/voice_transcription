How to Use

Upload an audio recording (sample has been provided) and press upload

The voice recognition model produces the transcript, which you can then review. Press okay to proceed

NER model indentifies the relevant entities and fills in form automatically. Edit according to need and save

To record audio, you can use arecord command in Ubuntu. It must be a mono channel, 16 bit, 16Khz recording
